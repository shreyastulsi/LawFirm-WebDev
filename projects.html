Non Profit Projects

Splash!

Our newest program, Services Preventing Los Angeles Slum Housing, is aimed at holding landlords accountable for poor living conditions in the residential complexes that they manage. Many issues currently plague tenants in Los Angeles like rats, cockroaches, faulty windows, water leaks, poor electrical installation, dust from construction, excessive noise, and other things that may constitute a nuisance or threaten the habitability of a housing unit. SPLASH! is entirely dedicated to enforcing the housing standards outlined by the city of Los Angeles, the state of California, and the Department of Housing and Urban Development. We make waves, so that tenants have the opportunity to enjoy their homes to the fullest extent protected by law.

Who Qualifies? Tenants who are suffering in uninhabitable housing conditions are eligible for representation if they can show a demonstrated economic need for legal help. We look at the entire circumstances of an individual or family’s financial circumstances and make determinations on a case-by-case basis.
